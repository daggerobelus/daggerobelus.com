Sarah Bonanno is an English PhD student studying early modern drama at the CUNY Graduate Center. Her dissertation will likely focus on theatrical representations of women's reproductive lives from virginity to childbirth. Sarah is also a Graduate Teaching Fellow in the English Department at Brooklyn College. She lives in Brooklyn, NY with her dog, Bella. 

Sarah graduated cum laude from Bowdoin College in 2018 where she received an BA in English with honors. She also holds a MA degree from the University of Chicago with a concentration in Theater and Performance Studies. 

Sarah can be reached at [sbonanno@gradcenter.cuny.edu](mailto:sbonanno@gradcenter.cuny.edu).
